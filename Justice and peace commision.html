JUSTICE AND PEACE COMMISION

The formation of this commission is a response of the diocese towards social problems that impinge human rights within and outside the diocesan boarders. The commission seeks to fight all forms of injustices through creation of awareness, lobbying and advocacy as well as establishing systems and structures for controlling injustices and thereby maintain peace, justice and reconciliation.
